Description:
This is a nice change from regular tomato sauce pasta. This is simple yet irresistable. You can serve a large portion as a main dish at dinner parties.

Ingredients
1. 8 ounces spiral pasta
2. 6 tablespoon olive oil
3. 2 cloves of sliced garlic
4. 8 tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
5. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
6. 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley

Servings: 2

Preparation Time:
Prep: 5 m
Cook: 15 m
Ready In: 20 m
